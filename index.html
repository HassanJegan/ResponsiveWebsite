Professional Maid Service & Commercial Janitors

Professionals' Choice provides housekeeping for your home as well as professional janitorial service for your business. We do all jobs, large and small. Our services include stripping and waxing floors, cleaning baseboards and refrigerators, making beds, carpet cleaning, and cleaning anything else that needs to be cleaned. We even do window cleaning! See our Services page for details.

We provide all of the cleaning supplies, travel to your home or place of business in company cars, and all of our housekeepers and janitors are bonded for your protection. We offer free estimates and accept all major credit cards as well as checks.

We provide maid service, home cleaning, and janitorial service to customers in Alvin, Manvel, Santa Fe, Friendswood, Pearland, Clear Lake, Webster, Texas City, Bacliff, Angleton, Lake Jackson, Danbury, Liverpool, Fresno, Arcola, Missouri City and many other towns and communities. To find out more about maid service and professional cleaning in your area, please contact us.
